Who We Are

Gender Frontier Development Initiatives (GFDI) is a grassroots advocacy organization committed to defending the human rights of vulnerable women and children in Nigeria. By confronting systemic abuse and harmful traditional practices head-on, we work to protect those most at risk in our communities.

Our campaigns directly challenge female genital mutilation, child labour, and human trafficking—bringing awareness, intervening in crisis situations, and promoting education as a pathway to empowerment. We believe that every child deserves a safe environment to grow, and every woman deserves the autonomy to shape her own future without fear of violence or exploitation.

The Issues We Fight

Our core campaigns address critical human rights violations.

Female Genital Mutilation

Campaigning against FGM through community education and zero-tolerance advocacy to protect the physical integrity of girls.

Say No to FGM

Child Labour

Intervening to protect minors forced into hazardous work, advocating for their absolute right to education.

Say No to Child Labour

Human Trafficking

Fighting the exploitation of human beings, prioritizing the protection of those lured into modern slavery networks.

Say No to Trafficking

Child Abuse

Defending innocence by providing advocacy and resource pathways for victims of systemic physical and emotional abuse.

Say No to Child Abuse

Rape & Gender Violence

Standing against gender-based violence, offering a voice to survivors, and demanding comprehensive support systems.

Say No to Rape

Women Abuse

Providing a safe haven and advocacy resources for women facing domestic violence and systemic abuse, empowering them to reclaim their lives.

Say No to Women Abuse
